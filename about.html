Hi, I'm Candi

I am a UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design and production. As a creative professional I am passionate about user focused design, I am organised and detail oriented with more than 20 years experience as a designer and production manager within the media. I love to create pleasing, clean, minimalist designs, understanding the user is at the heart of my design process through user research, usability testing. Specialising in information architecture, wireframing and prototyping, I create interfaces that bring users joy and increase brand loyalty. Having worked in large teams and small scale environments, I am well equipped to collaborate in different environments from large teams to remote working.

I love to create pleasing, clean, minimalist designs, I have a talent for organising content and whipping it into beautiful, elegant, user-focused interfaces, understanding the user is at the heart of everything I do.
